Davinci Profiles

Codes that represent the Da Vinci project set of profiles. The concept codes are equivalent to the StructureDefinition.id, the Display to the StructureDefinition. element and the defiinition are from the StructureDefinition.description

This code system http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-deqm/CodeSystem/davinci-profiles defines the following codes:

CodeDisplayDefinition
coverage-davinci Da Vinci Coverage ProfileThe Da Vinci Coverage Profile defines the constraints for representing the coverage where the Medication Reconciliation records are kept.
location-davinci Da Vinci Location ProfileThe Da Vinci Location Profile defines the constraints for representing the coverage id.
measurereport-mrp Da Vinci MeasureReport MRP ProfileThe Da Vinci MeasureReport MRP Profile is based on the Da Vinci MeasureReport Profile and constrains the `measure` element to medication reconciliation post-discharge and the `evaluatedResources` element to Task or Observation profiles.
measurereport-davinci Da Vinci MeasureReport ProfileThe Da Vinci MeasureReport Profile.
organization-davinci Da Vinci Organization ProfileThe Da Vinci Organization Profile defines the constraints for representing the organization whch is responsible for the Medication Reconciliation.
patient-davinci Da Vinci Patient ProfileThe Da Vinci Patient Profile defines the constraints for representing a patient who is the subject of a Medication Reconciliation.
practitioner-davinci Da Vinci Practitioner ProfileThe Da Vinci Practitioner Profile defines the constraints for representing the practitioner who is responsible for the Medication Reconciliation.
practitionerrole-davinci Da Vinci PractitionerRole ProfileThe Da Vinci PractitionerRole Profile defines the constraints for representing the role which is responsible for the Medication Reconciliation.
observation-mrp Da Vinci Observation MRP ProfileThe Da Vinci Observation MRP Profile is based upon the Da Vinci Observation Measure Profile and constrains the code(s) for representing the medication reconciliation post-discharge.
observation-measure Da Vinci Observation Measure ProfileThe Da Vinci Observation Measure Profile defines the constraints for representing the completion of a quality measure.
task-mrp Da Vinci Task MRP ProfileThe Da Vinci Task MRP Profile is based upon the Da Vinci Task Measure Profile and constrains the code(s) for representing the medication reconciliation post-discharge.
task-measure Da Vinci Task Measure ProfileThe Da Vinci Task Measure Profile defines the constraints for representing the completion of a provider quality measure.
