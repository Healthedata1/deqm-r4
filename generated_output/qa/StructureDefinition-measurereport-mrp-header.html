The Da Vinci MeasureReport MRP Profile is based on the Da Vinci MeasureReport Profile and represents the MRP quality measure results. It constrains the measure element to medication reconciliation post-discharge and the evaluatedResources element to Task or Observation profiles.

This profile builds on Da Vinci MeasureReport Profile.
